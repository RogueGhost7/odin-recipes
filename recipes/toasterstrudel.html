Cream Cheese Toaster Strudels

Home

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces cream cheese
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 6 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 sheet puff pastry
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 cup confectioners sugar
  • 1 tablespoon milk, more as needed

Directions

  1. Step 1: Place cream cheese in the freezer for 15 minutes before preparation.
  2. Step 2: Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  3. Step 3: Slice butter into 12 slices. Place 2 slices vertically in 6 sections on the prepared baking sheet. Top each set of butter slices with 1 tablespoon brown sugar.
  4. Step 4: Slice partially frozen cream cheese into 12 slices. Set 2 slices side by side on top of brown sugar sections.
  5. Step 5: Lightly roll puff pastry on a lightly floured surface. Cut pastry sheet into 6 rectangles. Press pastry around each mound of cream cheese mixture, pressing slightly down on the cream cheese. Use a fork to crimp the edges of each pastry. Brush pastries with beaten egg.
  6. Step 6: Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown and flaky, 15 to 18 minutes. Let stand on the baking sheet for 10 minutes before carefully turning over.
  7. Step 7: Meanwhile, whisk together confectioners sugar and milk until smooth. Drizzle desired amount of icing over pastries.
